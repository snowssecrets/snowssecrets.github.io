Nata De Coco
(Biotechnology Product)


Ingredients: Tools:
  • 25 gram of Sugar
  • 5 gram of Urea Substance
  • 9 ml of starter Nata de Coco
  • 1 liter of coconut water
  • 10 ml of vinegar
  • Pan
  • Table Spoon
  • Pan
  • Measuring Cup
  • Plastic Container
  • Duct Tape
  • Newspaper
  • Strainer
Steps:
  1. First, start by preparing your tools and ingredients.
  2. Pour your strained coconut water into the pan. The coconut water is cooked until it boils.
  3. After the coconut water boils, add the sugar, vinegar, and urea substance inside the coconut water.The coconut water mixture is referred to as the mixture.
  4. Then, throw out the foam that appears on the surface of the boiled coconut water.
  5. Remove the pan and pour the hot coconut water mixture into the prepared plastic container.
  6. Place the prepared newspaper as a cover for the plastic container. The newspaper cover is sealed tightly with duct tape.
  7. Wait for the coconut water mixture to cool until approximately 30 degrees Celsius or room temperature.
  8. Cooling down of the coconut water has been completed. After that, open the newspaper cover and pour in the starter solution without mixing it in.
  9. Reattach the newspaper cover onto the plastic container. Make sure it's closed tightly so no dirt or dust gets inside.
  10. Let the mixture sit for about 7 days.
  11. After 7 days, the mixture will form into a sheet of nata de coco.
  12. The nata de coco is ready for consumption.

Strawberry Milo Pudding
(Cooking product)


Ingredients: Tools:
  • 2 sachet of milo
  • 400 ml of full cream milk
  • 2 spoon of sugar
  • 3 spoon of condensed milk
  • 1 strawberry for garnish
  • 1 spoon of cornflour
  • 1 sachet of nutrijell
  • 300 ml of water
  • Spatula
  • Measuring Cup
  • Pan
  • Basque
  • Serving plate
Steps:
  1. Prepare a pan, add nutrijell, milo, sugar, milk, and condensed milk. The pudding liquid needs to be stirred until smooth. After it’s smooth, turn on the stove over low heat, and keep stirring it.
  2. Combine water and cornflour, stir it until smooth, and set aside first.
  3. Wait until the pudding’s solution boils and bubbles appear. Add the cornflour that has been combined with water and stir it. The pudding liquid is going to be dissolved after a few minutes of stirring.
  4. Pour the pudding into a serving bowl and strainer it. Then, put it into the refrigerator. The pudding had to become dense perfectly so it could be delicious.
  5. While waiting for the pudding to dense, clean the kitchen and also the tools.
  6. After the pudding becomes dense, prepare the garnish. We chose strawberry and milo as the garnish. The strawberry is cut and placed on top of the pudding. The milo is sprinkled on the top of the pudding, and so is the strawberry.
  7. The pudding is ready to be served and eaten!
